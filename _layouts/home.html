Who are we?

We are an innovative engineering company providing integrated and wholesome solutions for the Acoustics, Pro Audio/Video, Lighting and FM/Internet Radio broadcasting. In these domains, we provide consultancy and projection execution services.

Ours is a Private Limited company incorporated by the Ministry of Corporate affairs, Government of India under the companies law 1956 (No. 1 of 1956).

“Varpas” - a Sanskrit word of Vedic origin meaning “image” or “design” signifies the company’s effort to help our client in the preparation and delivery of quality aural and visual content for effective communication with their subjects.

Incidentally, the word, Varpas represents the sectors we operate - Video, Audio, Radio, Product development, Acoustics, and Surveillance.

Varpas is a team of committed,qualified and experienced professionals working in sync with the state-of-the-art technology for delivering the optimum, economical and professional solutions for their clients.

Domains

Varpas offers its professional acoustics, Audio, Video and RF broadcast engineering services in the following sectors:

  • Auditoriums
  • Seminar halls, board/meeting rooms etc
  • e-Classrooms
  • Radio broadcast facilities for both community radio and commercial private FM.
  • Internet radio and podcasting
  • Workflow for educational TV studios and post production facilities.
  • CCTV and surveillance
  • Home theatres

Our Solutions

Varpas provides end-to-end turnkey solution for the establishment of solutions and systems for content creation, professional audio, video, broadcast and allied sectors. It starts from understanding the idea and requirement of the client, to translate it as a solution, designing a system to meet the solution, advisory on choice of equipments so that, client can get value for their investment, supply of equipments and other material, installation, testing and commissioning of the system, training on operation and maintenance of the installed system. Varpas always had been a holding hand for its clients which support them to achieve the envisaged solution in an optimized and professional way comprehending their business of operation. Below are listing of the services offered by Varpas.

  • Preparation of Detailed Project Report containing detailed technical specification, bill of quantities, drawings, tender documents etc as per the organizational requirements.
  • Site preparatory works viz civil/electrical/HVAC engineering works as per the site requirement and providing design and drawings for the work execution.
  • Studio design and Design audio/video/IT work flow for radio/TV stations as per the client's requirement.
  • RF broadcast systems and network distribution through cable and Internet.
  • All works related with the Auditorium interiors viz Acoustics, Installed audio, Stage and FoH lighting system, Proscenium box, Drapery, Seating, Cat walk structure etc for auditoriums.
  • Video recording/presentation/distribution, switching and presentation systems, Collaborative platforms like, video conferencing, streaming, wireless presentation systems, Digital signage system etc.
  • Acoustic design, treatment and validation by measurements as per ISO 3382 standard.
  • President delegate system with voting, interpretation, look at me option etc., suiting the conference applications for board rooms, legislative assemblies etc.
  • CCTV for surveillence, Fire alarm and fire fighting equipment.
  • Electrical power distribution and general purpose lighting systems.
  • Customized furniture for the interiors, class room, auditoriums and other application.
  • Third party inspection and documentation for works of above nature
  • Advisory on Government policy on Radio/TV broadcasting and distribution by satellite/Cable, guidance in licensing process etc.
